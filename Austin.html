Founded in 1839, the capital city of Texas is located in the north central portion of the state. The urban city has become a hub for technology companies and startups. Austin hosts a thriving musical scene, even earning the name of “The Capital of Live Music”. due to this the population has grown to 1 million with an average income of $65,000 a year. Wether you want to enjoy some BBQ, check out the Texas History museum or to just float the river, Austin has it for you.
