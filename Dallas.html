Dallas was founded in 1841 and is also located in north central Texas. Just a 2 hours drive north of Austin, Dallas Ft Worth is one of the largest airports in the United States. If you love sports you can catch a Rangers game or drive to the neighboring city Arlington to catch a Cowboys game. BBQ fans will have to stop at Terry Blacks for some good eats. The median household income of the 1.3 million residents is comperable to the other major cities at $63,000.
